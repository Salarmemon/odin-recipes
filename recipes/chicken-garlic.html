Chicken Garlic

Description

flavorful, savory chicken dish cooked in a rich garlic-infused sauce. It’s aromatic, slightly buttery, and perfect to pair with rice, noodles, or flatbread.

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken (boneless or with bone)
  • 6–8 garlic cloves (finely chopped)
  • 2 tbsp oil or butte
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce (optional)
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp chili flakes (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth or water
  • 1 tsp cornstarch (optional, for thickening)
  • Spring onions (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Heat oil or butter in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add chopped garlic and sauté until fragrant (don’t burn it).
  3. Add the chicken pieces and cook until lightly browned.
  4. Stir in soy sauce, oyster sauce, salt, pepper, and chili flakes.
  5. Pour in chicken broth/water, cover, and let it cook for 10–15 minutes until chicken is tender.
  6. Cook for another 2–3 minutes until sauce is glossy.
  7. Garnish with spring onions and serve hot.
